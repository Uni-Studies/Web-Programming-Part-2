Add Paging to the Users Management listing page UsersList.jsx
Paging settngs should contain:
 - Page
 - ItemsPerPage

Pages should be displayed in the form of links or buttons.
Items per page should be in the form of a dropdown list.
When the items per page selection changes the new settings should be applied and the data should be refreshed.

Paging information can be extracted from the data.pager property of the response:
